The Story



The Last Horizon is a science fiction game that takes players on an epic adventure on an unknown planet. The protagonist, Malika, experiences a brutal crash of her spaceship due to engine failure. She finds herself stranded alone on a strange and hostile world. As she explores, she discovers the ruins of an abandoned laboratory, where she accidentally activates an interdimensional portal, threatening to invade her planet with creatures from another dimension. To prevent this invasion, she must close the portal before it's too late. Throughout her journey, Malika will need to activate generators, access crucial databases, face deadly threats, and uncover secrets that could change the fate of humanity. In the end, she must launch a rocket to escape to a space station, but her mission is just the beginning of a new adventure. Get ready for a thrilling journey where survival, discovery, and action are at the heart of every decision.
